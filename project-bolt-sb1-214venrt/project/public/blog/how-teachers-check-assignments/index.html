How Teachers Check Assignments

2026-07-04

Completeness beats decoration

Teachers look for correct answers and full steps first. Fancy pages cannot replace missing content.

Neat margins and spacing help readability

Crowded lines are harder to mark. Previewing on ruled paper helps you see if answers look cramped.
